We are the Leader in Engineering Industry

C-J Engineering delivers specialised refractory engineering and supply solutions across Africa, partnering local responsiveness with global technology. We design, specify and support high-temperature linings and wear-resistant systems—focusing on monolithic and shaped refractories, thermal-efficiency upgrades and full lining solutions that maximise uptime and reduce operating cost. As the regional partner to world-leading refractory suppliers, C-J Engineering combines proven materials and engineering know-how with hands-on site services: lining design, installation supervision, shutdown and trial support, material selection and technical training. Our team works closely with plant engineers to tailor solutions for copper and platinum smelting, cement and lime, non-ferrous metals, power generation and other high-temperature process industries. We’re committed to practical engineering, measurable results and long-term partnerships—delivering safer, more efficient furnaces and process equipment through expert design, quality products and reliable on-site support.
